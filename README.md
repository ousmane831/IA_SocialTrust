**Project Title:**
Explainable AI for Fair and Transparent Social Assistance

---

**Project Concept Brief**

Across many developing and emerging countries, access to social assistance programs remains a critical challenge. Citizens often face opaque decision-making processes when applying for public aid such as healthcare coverage, financial support, or family assistance programs. Decisions are frequently perceived as unfair or biased, leading to distrust in institutions and exclusion of vulnerable populations.

In Senegal, for instance, programs like CMU (Couverture Maladie Universelle), PNBSF (Programme National de Bourses de Sécurité Familiale), and family scholarships aim to support low-income households. However, the lack of transparency in eligibility decisions and the absence of clear explanations for acceptance or rejection create frustration and reduce public confidence in these systems.

To address this issue, we propose **SunuAISocialTrust**, an AI-powered platform designed to make social assistance systems more transparent, fair, and trustworthy.

SunuAISocialTrust leverages machine learning models to evaluate eligibility for social programs based on socio-economic data. What differentiates our solution is its strong focus on explainability, fairness, and security. Instead of providing a simple “eligible” or “not eligible” result, the system generates clear and understandable explanations for each decision. This allows citizens to understand why a decision was made, increasing transparency and trust.

The platform integrates explainable AI techniques that highlight the key factors influencing each decision, such as income level, household size, or geographic conditions. These explanations are simplified so that even non-technical users can easily understand them. In addition, the system includes bias detection mechanisms to monitor and reduce unfair treatment based on gender, region, or other sensitive attributes.

From a user perspective, the process is simple: a citizen submits a request through an intuitive interface, selects the desired social program, and provides relevant information. The AI system processes the data, evaluates eligibility, and returns a decision along with a clear explanation and a confidence score. If needed, the case can be flagged for human review, ensuring accountability.

Security is also a core component of the platform. All sensitive data is protected using strong encryption standards, ensuring privacy and compliance with modern data protection principles.

The impact of SunuAISocialTrust is significant. It empowers citizens by giving them visibility into decision-making processes, reduces administrative inefficiencies, and strengthens trust between governments and the population. By improving fairness and transparency, the system contributes to more inclusive and equitable public services.

Furthermore, the solution is designed to be scalable and adaptable. While initially focused on Senegal, it can be extended to other countries and adapted to different types of public assistance programs. Its modular architecture allows integration with existing government systems, making deployment feasible in real-world environments.

In alignment with the goals of inclusive and secure AI, SunuAISocialTrust demonstrates how technology can be used responsibly to address real societal challenges. It combines innovation, ethical AI practices, and practical usability to create a system that not only makes decisions but also explains them—ensuring that no citizen is left in the dark.
